About Ginger

Ginger’s favorite thing in the world to do is encourage parents to reach the hearts of their children for the glory of God. Ginger and her husband, Ronnie, are empty nesters now, so they love traveling and ministering together at parenting events and homeschool conventions across the country.

Ginger is fresh, funny, and full of wisdom! Her advice is overflowing with God’s instruction and grace, and her personal stories will have you laughing one minute and wiping tears the next. - Lysa Terkeurst
